What is the best Food in the World?

Quite a good question, huh? What do I mean, when i'm talking about the best food in the world. Does it mean best-tasting, most enjoyable or just the best food for body and soul? Well, wouldn't it be great to have all in one? I truely believe that we can get all of these pleasures at once. Most important of all is that we love handling the food, we are eating. It should be fun preparing tasty meals and the portions should be big enough, so we don't have to restrict ourselves. Being hungry all the time can be very exhausting. Therefore we want foods which are not that calory-dense, taste good and satisfy our needs of nutrients. Depending on our activity-rate throughout the day its is appropriate to add more calory-dense but still healthy energy sources, so we don't have to eat for the most time we are not active.
